I am a mathematician at the <a href="https://www.mimuw.edu.pl/en/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics and Mechanics</a> of the University of Warsaw. Starting from October 2026 I will hold there the position of Samuel Eilenberg Assistant Professor. Currently, I am a postdoc in a research project led by <a href="https://www.mimuw.edu.pl/~bmia/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Błażej Miasojedow</a>.

I completed my PhD in Warsaw, advised by <a href="https://www.il.pw.edu.pl/en/member/lewinski-tomasz-en/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Tomasz Lewiński</a> and <a href="https://www.mimuw.edu.pl/~rybka/index.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Piotr Rybka</a>. <a href="https://theses.hal.science/tel-05609664v1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">My thesis</a> concerned the optimal design of engineering structures. My mathematical perspective on these topics has been shaped by the works of <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/gbouchitte/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Guy Bouchitté</a>, a dear collaborator of mine ever since before my defense.
